About me

Hello, my name is Linzy Rose. I am currently enrolled at the University of Arizona, working towards a certification of completion for coding and web development. Attending a program like this, opens a lot of doors career wise, which is truly exciting.

I graduated from Northern Arizona University in 2018 with a bachelors in psychology. I loved learning about the brain, body language, and the field over all.

I was in the restaurant industry for roughly 7 years. It was a true love hate relationship. I loved the atmosphere and the flexible schedule when attending school. But the service industry can be rough on the soul. Bartending is something I truly enjoy doing, but not as a main source of income.

As for coding goes: I am creative, detailed oriented, very much a beginner, but determined to be great at it.
